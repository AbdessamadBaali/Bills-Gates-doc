What does preset mean in editing?
Description: Presets are edits or adjustments that have been saved for use in editing software (Lightroom, Capture One, etc.) to recreate a certain look in one click. Presets represent one of the most powerful post-production tools for simplifying the post production process.
What are presets and how do you use them?
A preset allows you to create a certain style or aesthetic with a single click while in the develop module. Presets can enhance or apply hues, shadows, colors, contrast, or grain. You can change one image or, this is the best part of using presets, a batch of images, saving you loads of workflow time.
